The original glTF mesh/skeleton was converted into GeckoLib's bone +
poly_mesh format (`cat_bot_cat_mesh.geo.json`) for use in this mod; the
diffuse texture is used unmodified. The original box-based placeholder
model (`cat_bot_cat.geo.json`, `cat_bot_cat.png`) remains in the repo,
unused, and is original WorldForge project art (no third-party license
applies to it).
